who is she?

Hi, my name is Annabelle. I like to think of adventure in a quiet way. To me, adventure is a compilation of stories, photographs, and songs that have been collected throughout a journey. It is probably why I've saved all my flight tickets and hospital bracelets and endless movie stubs.

This website is a collage of my adventures. It is an online scrapbook showcasing my personal and professional photography sessions and web design projects. You'll also find some DIY inspiration for whatever new hobby I might be learning and a whole lot of puppy love to go around.

I'm working my way up to more adventurous adventures, although I do enjoy having a solid lazy Sunday and cozying up with a book.
